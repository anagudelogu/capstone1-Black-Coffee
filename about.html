Black Coffee Fest is a fantasy electronic dance music festival series. with its Colombia edition taking place on April 20th, with a number of international editions held across various territories worldwide.
First held in 1998 in Winchester, the festival moved to Liverpool the following year, and have been switching locations from all over the world since then. The festival having initially begun as a one-day event with 25,000 people in attendance, is now a four-day event with camping options hosting 200,000 per day.
